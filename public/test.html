Restaurant: Biga on the Banks

Address: 203 S St Mary's St, San Antonio, Texas 78205, USA

Chef/Owner Bruce Auden and Perny Shea, Director of Special Events, have created a relaxed, come-as-you-are ambience, offering world-class New American cuisine and expert service. Their wine cellar offers a deep and varied selection of wines to pair brilliantly with their cuisine while overlooking the San Antonio Riverwalk. To learn more about Biga read their blog, The Talk.
